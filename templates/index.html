About Agentic Scaffolding

Agentic Scaffolding is a meta-system or architectural layer designed to support, coordinate, and enhance the capabilities of individual AI agents or multi-agent systems within an ecosystem. It provides the necessary structure, services, and environment that facilitate the achievement of complex, high-level goals which might be beyond the capacity of individual agents operating in isolation.

Key Components:
  • Goal and Task Decomposition: Breaking down complex goals into manageable subtasks
  • Planning and Sequencing: Determining the optimal order of execution for subtasks
  • Dynamic Coordination: Allocating tasks to appropriate agents and monitoring progress
  • Resource Management: Providing access to knowledge bases, tools, and APIs
  • Communication: Enabling standardized interaction between agents

This demonstration showcases how these components work together to process a high-level goal through a team of specialized agents.
